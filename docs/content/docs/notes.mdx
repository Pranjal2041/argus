title: Notes Hub

Not everything is a to-do. The **Notes Hub** is one place for free-form notes — ideas, snippets,
reminders — that aren't tied to any machine or session. It is the plain-writing companion to
[Todo Maps](/docs/todo-maps): todos are checklists bound to a session; notes are just notes.

## What it does

- **One list of notes**, each **multiline** (notes get long) and optionally **checkable**.
- **Grouped by time** — *Today*, *Yesterday*, *Earlier this week*, *This month*, *Earlier* — newest
  first, ordered by when you **last edited** each note.
- A **full-width** panel, the same shape as the Command Center and Todo Maps.

<Callout type="info">
Open it with `⇧⌘N` on macOS, or the notes tab on the phone. Notes are **synced across your devices**
(see [Architecture](/docs/architecture#user-data-sync-your-mac-is-the-sync-host)), and edits don't
hit the network on every keystroke, so long notes stay smooth.
</Callout>
